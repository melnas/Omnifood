About Us

Omni Foods is an organization that works to feed the needy of India who is deprived of good food. Despite adequate food production, the UN has reported that about 190 million Indians remain undernourished. India wastes up to 67 million tonnes of food every year, according to a government study. As per Hindustan Times, the value of the food lost is Rs 92,000 crore. Omni Food serves as a bridge to connect the hotel managements, party organisers and every common Indian citizen who desire to donate their extra food to the superintendents of orphanages, hospitals, old-age homes, and other social service organisations.
